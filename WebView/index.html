Get a week's worth of
work done in a day.

There's no faster way to arrive at an effective group solution than through a design studio. It's a short but intense.

see the steps below

Step 1. Give 'em context

People. Sketchpads. Markers. Just as important: Context. Give everyone the basics on product, competitors, marketplace and target audience. Avoid cat videos unless your product is cat-related. Do this in 30 minutes or less.

Step 2. Define your challenge

Distill the problem you are trying to solve into a short sentence that focuses on a product feature and the audience who will engage with it. Post this challenge so everyone can see it. Hurry people along. 5 minutes.

Step 3. Speed-date your ideas

Break your team into groups of 5 people. Give each group its own space. Everyone has 5 minutes to roughly sketch 5 solutions to the challenge. That’s right, only 5 minutes! Don’t go deep, go broad.

Step 4. Show your cards

Each group member presents their sketches to their group. (The whole team reassembles later.) Groups critique, refine and/or dismiss their ideas to get to the top 3. Be brutal: you have only 25 minutes.

Step 5. Do it again and again.

Each group does two additional sequences of sketching, presenting and discussing -- but are still limited to keeping only the best 3 ideas at the end. Tough. 60 minutes

Step 6. Avengers, assemble!

Your whole team gets back together. Every group presents their top 3 ideas. Everyone votes on the which idea wins. Fortunes are won and lost, but you look like a genius.
